Frequently Asked Questions

How do I invest?

Create an account, choose a plan, deposit, and your earnings begin automatically.

When do I receive my earnings?

Earnings are calculated daily based on your selected plan and appear in your dashboard.

Can I withdraw anytime?

Withdrawals are available based on plan duration and processing time. Daily withdrawals may apply.

Is my data safe?

Yes. We use secure Firebase authentication and protect all user data with encryption protocols.
